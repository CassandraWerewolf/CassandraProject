This is a 7 player game.

[b]Team evil:[/b]

Evil wins at wolf parity.

[u]2 Werewolves:[/u] Kill 1 player each night. Have a chat room.

[u]Sorcerer:[/u] Looks for the seer each night. Chooses a N0 view.


[b]Team Good:[/b]

Team good wants the wolves dead.

[u]Seer:[/u] looks for the wolves each night. Chooses a N0 view.

[u]Hunter:[/u] defeats a wolf if left alone with one.

[u]Priest:[/u] gets a view of a dead player's role each night.

[u]Villager:[/u] tasty wolf treat.

[b]Standard BGG werewolf rules apply otherwise.[/b]
